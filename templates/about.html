about Tweetspiration

Tired of brainstorming awesome app ideas? Let Twitter do it for you!

What is this website?

Tweetspiration scours the web for Twitter users' wishes, desires, and complaints. It may provide some inspiration if you're looking for an idea for an app (or website or business). Just sit back and let the internet brainstorm for you!

Caution: none of these tweets are screened, so there may occasionally be an unsuitable message. View at your own risk :)

How does it work?

Periodically, we run this search on Twitter and collect the results. We then do a bunch of fun stuff to these tweets and display them here! It operates on Google App Engine, Python style.
